Introduction to what we do


Engen is a major African energy company that supplies and distributes refined petroleum products, lubricants, and chemicals. They operate over 1,000 service stations in sub-Saharan Africa, offering fuels (petrol, diesel, jet fuel), high-performance lubricants, and retail convenience services


We deal with supplying fuel to all the vehicles and to make sure that everyone is safe in the road by providing fuel so they can keep on moving to their destinations
