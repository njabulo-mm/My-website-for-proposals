Our History

The history of engine supply has evolved from the early, localized production of steam engines in the 18th century to the highly complex, globalized, and specialized supply chains for internal combustion engines (ICE) and electric motors today. This evolution was driven by the need for greater efficiency, reliability, and power-to-weight ratios for transportation.
